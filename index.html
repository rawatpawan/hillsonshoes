about us

We stand for quality, innovation and direct customer relationships

WE ARE A LEADING MANUFACTURER, SUPPLIER AND EXPORTER OF SAFETY SHOES, NON-SAFETY PVC BOOTS, SAFETY GUMBOOTS, ETC. AT AFFORDABLE PRICE.

Hillson started from a small unit with a vision to stand out and fulfill the demands of the consumers. Being one of the largest manufacturers of safety shoes in India, Hillson caters to a broad spectrum of industries. With a team of highly skilled professionals, they aim to provide quality products that are tested and certified by reputed laboratories. Hillson majorly focuses on – Durability, Affordability, and Value for Money. The objective is to add value to the customer’s life with products that Hillson manufactures. It’s not just a company anymore; it’s a family who has been together through thick and thin.
