About Us

We stand for quality, innovation and direct customer relationships

Hillson, established in 1980 by Mr. Rambabu Agarwal, has evolved to become one of the largest manufacturers and exporters of Industrial Safety Shoes and Gumboots, embodying values of integrity, resilience, and passion. Hillson’s journey is now driven forward with the vision of his sons, Mr Nitin Agarwal and Mr Sachin Agarwal who have transformed it into a global leader in safety footwear.

Hillson’s extensive range of footwear includes Safety Footwear and Gumboots, with options tailored for both men and women. These safety shoes and gumboots are crafted with reinforced toes and durable materials, providing essential protection from various workplace hazards. Engineered to meet stringent safety standards, Hillson footwear is vital Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) in industrial and construction sites, safeguarding workers from risks such as airborne particles, hazardous chemicals, electrical hazards, heavy objects, and biohazards.

Today, Hillson’s new presentation in the market symbolizes the brand’s commitment to innovation and ambition to elevate safety standards globally, while staying grounded in the values that have guided them from the start.

PROUD MOMENTS OF
HILLSON SHOES

Driving Industry Growth

1980-89

A son coming from the beautiful city of hills, Mr Rambabu Agarwal laid the foundation for the shoes business on the pillars of Integrity, customer- centricity, and care for people. Though the business started small, the vision for the future was big. The iconic long gumboot WELCOME launched in 1986 proved to be a turning point, for Hillson to expand rapidly in the safety market.

1990-99

To expand their market from manufacturing only men gumboots to ladies and kids, the elder son, Mr Nitin Agarwal, along-side his education, joined the business to learn manufacturing. His aspirations took a flight and he travelled around the world to envision the growth of Hillson.

2000-09

Soon his younger sibling, Mr Sachin Agarwal, took the responsibility of the sales and marketing department in the business. After initial learning, the young men started taking leaps of faith one after the other. In 2005, a manufacturing facility was established in Bahadurgarh, where advanced step machines were imported to produce safety shoes and steel-toe gumboots. These efforts marked a shift toward ambitious growth adhering to global safety standards.

2010-19

In their pursuit of expanding market share, Hillson made a strategic decision to establish a manufacturing facility in Bahadurgarh. This move allowed the company to scale operations and meet the growing demand for high-quality footwear. The factory contributed significantly to Hillson's ability to dominate the safety footwear market.

2024

Expanding the product range, enhancing manufacturing capabilities, increasing market share, and strengthening the team transformed the Bahadurgarh factory into a facility with an installed capacity of over 25,000 pairs of footwear per day. With this solid foundation, Hillson set its sights on innovation, realizing it was time to embrace something new and experience the magic.
